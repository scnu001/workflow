# Operational Actionability Gate

## Purpose

Operational products must produce actions, not just analysis.

## Required Fields Per Important Finding

- what to do
- why it matters
- priority
- expected impact
- risk
- required input or resource
- next step
- export/copy/action path

## Inventory Output Requirements

- reorder quantity
- vendor
- estimated spend
- priority rank
- stockout risk
- overstock risk
- budget-constrained recommendation
- what to delay if budget is limited

## Rules

- Generic recommendations block `TOP_10_PUBLISH`.
- If the product only reports problems and does not guide action, downgrade.
